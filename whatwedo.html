Involvements
The Marine Wildlife Watch of the Philippines is actively involved in diverse initiatives for marine ecosystem protection. They conduct regular surveillance and monitoring, combat illegal fishing through anti-poaching operations, and engage in community education for awareness about marine biodiversity. Supporting the establishment of Marine Protected Areas, the organization conducts scientific research, contributes to rescue efforts for injured marine animals, and advocates for enhanced regulations through collaboration with government agencies. Their comprehensive efforts reflect a dedicated commitment to marine wildlife conservation.
Activities And participation
The MWWP achieves its goals through projects and activities such as information dissemination, education and awareness raising campaigns, training, research, publication of knowledge products, policy and consultation work related to marine wildlife conservation. Providing relevant information online through various social media platforms, particularly Facebook, remains the main venue for information dissemination.
