Vision and Mission

Vision: To envision a thriving and sustainable marine environment in the Philippines, where diverse marine species flourish, ecosystems are resilient, and coastal communities coexist harmoniously with nature.

Mission: To support the conservation and protection of marine wildlife species and their habitats in the Philippines partnerships and collaborative work, remains unchanged.
